About

Design Meets Education

As a design technician, I combine creative design with workshop experience. Since 2023, I've been leading creative workshops for youth organizations – from graffiti to podcast production and beatmaking.

My practice includes mobile beatmaking workshops for 10-14 year olds (iPad/Caustic3), radio and moderation formats, as well as graphic design for youth projects.

Whether one-time project days or workshop series: The concepts are ready, the equipment is here. Let's discuss your next creative project!

What I Offer

Workshops for Youth Work

Media education workshops for youth organizations, schools and educational institutions: Graffiti & Lettering, Beatmaking & Music Production (iPad/Caustic3), Podcast & Radio Moderation, Graphic & Layout Basics. Single sessions, project days or workshop series – including technology and materials on request.

Graphic Design & Print Support

Poster designs, flyers, stickers, merchandise motifs – from the first idea to the finished product in your hands. I create print-ready files and guide you through the entire production process: getting quotes, coordinating correction loops, checking quality. You don't just get designs, but working print products.

Individual Projects

From illustrations to social media graphics to experimental formats – contact me for your creative project! I'm also the right person for campaign support: concept, project management and visual implementation all in one.
