About

Design Meets Education

As a design technician, I combine creative design with workshop experience. Since 2023, I've been leading creative workshops for youth organizations and independent institutions – from graffiti to podcast production and beatmaking.

My practice includes mobile beatmaking workshops for young people (with iPad and Caustic 3), radio and moderation formats, as well as graphic design for youth projects (with Clip Studio Paint and Affinity Suite).

Whether for project days or your design commission:
Feel free to reach out!
